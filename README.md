# HR-ANALTICS-PROJECT
End-to-end HR Analytics project using SQL, Excel, Tableau &amp; Power BI
Objective

- Identify key factors affecting employee attrition
- Analyze workforce trends and performance
- Build interactive dashboards for HR decision-making

🔄 **Workflow**

Excel → SQL → Tableau → Power BI

- Data cleaning & KPI planning (Excel)
- KPI calculation & validation (SQL)
- Visual analysis (Tableau)
- Final dashboard & insights (Power BI)

📊**Key KPIs**

- Attrition rate by department
- Attrition vs monthly income
- Average working years by department
- Job role vs work-life balance
- Attrition vs years since last promotion

🔍 **Key Insights**

- Certain departments show higher attrition rates
- Lower income groups have higher attrition
- Employees with longer promotion gaps are more likely to leave
- Work-life balance varies across job roles

🏁 **Conclusion**

Income level, promotion delays, experience, and job role are major factors influencing employee attrition.
Interactive dashboards help HR teams identify issues early and take action.
